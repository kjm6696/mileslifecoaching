Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our coaching services and how we can help you on your journey.

Life coaching is a process that helps individuals achieve their personal and professional goals through guidance, support, and accountability.

Life Coaching is for those who want change in their life. Life Coaching helps people get “unstuck” in life and achieve their true potential. Whether it is a change in relationships, career or everyday life, Miles Life Coaching can help!

In Life Coaching Sessions, we will explore intentions, set goals, expand skills and set manageable action steps to help each client move in the right direction. Life coaching sessions are dynamic and tailored to the individual client, focusing on empowering them to take charge of their life and achieve their goals.

Life Coaching is geared toward the client, so sessions vary as clients do.
